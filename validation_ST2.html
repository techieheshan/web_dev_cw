Reflection on the Validation Process

The meta-refresh tag on the Splash Screen initially failed validation with content="4;url=home.html" — the validator expects a space after the semicolon (content="4; url=home.html"). A small syntax detail, but one that would have silently broken the redirect in stricter browsers.

The AIS page's ARIA button issue (described above) was the more instructive fix: it's a reminder that ARIA roles come with real content restrictions, not just visual/behavioural implications.
